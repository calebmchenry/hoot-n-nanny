# Hoot 'n Nanny

Inspired by UFO 50's "Party House", but animal themed.

Hoot is a reference to an owl
Nanny is a reference to a nanny goat

Push your luck deck builder where you bust if your animals wake up the farm owner by being too noisy or exceed the barns capacity.

this will be a browser app. Should be available on github pages. We should make sure the the behavior and amount of variety is similar to the UFO 50's party house. Where farm related creatures are going to be the cards. With a variety of abilities. We should evaluate how reviews feel about the existing ablities in party house. Create new ones based on player interest and remove the things that don't work so well. Abilities should pair well together due to achieving a variety of tactics. 

Fun claissic pixel art. Classic retro sounds. For user inputs and animals, etc.

I should be able to go to the website on my phone and play a game as well as on a computer. Pass n' play multiplayer is a must. And single player should be an option too.
